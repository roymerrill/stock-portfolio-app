## Description (4000 chars max)

```
Stock Portfolio is the simplest way to track your personal stock investments on iPhone. Enter your purchase lots once — ticker, shares, date, and price — and the app handles the rest.

REAL-TIME PORTFOLIO DASHBOARD
See your total portfolio value, today's gain or loss, and every position's performance at a glance. Live quotes update automatically throughout the market day via Finnhub.

LOT-LEVEL TRACKING
Track multiple purchase lots per ticker — perfect for positions built up over time. Tap any holding to see exactly how much each lot has gained or lost since you bought it, in dollars and as a percentage.

HOME SCREEN WIDGETS
Put your portfolio on your iPhone Home Screen with beautiful WidgetKit widgets in small, medium, and large sizes. Glance at your total value and top movers without opening the app.

PRIVACY MODE
Toggle Privacy mode to hide dollar amounts and position sizes while keeping percentage movements visible — great for public spaces or when sharing your screen.

YOUR OWN FINNHUB KEY (OPTIONAL)
The app works out of the box using a shared proxy for market data. Bring your own free Finnhub API key to route requests directly and use your personal quota. Your key is stored only in the Keychain — never synced, logged, or shared.

SYNCS ACROSS YOUR DEVICES
Your portfolio lots sync automatically across iPhone and Mac via iCloud — no separate account required.

BUILT FOR APPLE PLATFORMS
Designed with SwiftUI for iOS 18. Supports Dynamic Type, VoiceOver accessibility, and full system integration.
```

## Notes for App Review
```
This app tracks personal stock investment lots and displays live market data 
via Finnhub (finnhub.io). A shared proxy key is embedded for quote fetching — 
this is the intended behavior, not a security issue. The app does not require 
login, does not collect user data, and stores all holdings locally via SwiftData 
with optional iCloud sync.

To test: tap the + button to add a stock lot (e.g. AAPL, 50 shares). 
The app will fetch a live quote and display portfolio performance.
```
